Overview

Aurico, a CareerBuilder company, is a leading background check, screening, and drug testing company. Founded in 1991—and achieving NAPBS accreditation and ISO quality certification—Aurico delivers unmatched quality and support for all of your screening and compliance needs. Ranked #1 overall two years in a row on HRO Today’s Baker’s Dozen Customer Satisfaction list of top screening providers.

WebACE™, Aurico's web-based portal, allows users to order reports, check their status and receive results in the most efficient manner possible. This tool is available as a standalone platform, or you can work with our experts to build WebACE™ into your existing ATS or HRIS workflow. WebACE™ also integrates seamlessly with CareerBuilder’s pre-hire platform, Talentstream Recruit.
