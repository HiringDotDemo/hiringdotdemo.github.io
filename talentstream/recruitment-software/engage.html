Overview

Talentstream Engage is an optimized career site to allow for easy job seeker interaction on any device. Designed to decrease candidate drop-off and increase applications, our recruitment tool takes candidate interaction to the next level.

Once the job seeker submits a resume or subscribes your company's talent network, he or she is automatically enrolled to receive new, open positions as they become available. With some of the highest email open and click-through rates in the industry, CareerBuilder’s proprietary matching technology ensures that you maximize your recruitment investment by helping passive candidates remain engaged and apply when the right role presents itself.

Whether you need to hire candidates around the corner or across the globe, with more than 4,000 career sites and talent network members in nearly every country, Talentstream Engage is the solution to build your talent pipeline for the future. ​​​​​​​

Features



​

Optimized Career Site

Drive candidates to search, join your talent network and apply for jobs on a career site built using 20 years of insider research, optimized for any device.
​

Talent Network

Build your talent pipeline through applicants and candidates not ready to apply by allowing them to join your talent network in just a few clicks. 

Automated Email Re-Engagement

Deliver new, relevant open positions to your talent network with automated emails powered by CareerBuilder's proprietary matching technology.


​

Texting

Allow job seekers to join your talent network with Text to Join, then communicate to your members with two way text messaging via TextRecruit.
​

Premium Design

Create a fully customized career site that showcases your employment brand and initiatives with Talentstream Engage’s Premium edition.​
​​

Global Reach

Powering 4,400 career sites in 30 languages to deliver your message around the world.​
