Overview

Talentstream Recruit is the intuitive candidate applicant tracking system you need to effectively attract quality talent and operate internal recruitment processes.

Our software takes you through the recruitment process from the moment a recruiter creates a requisition to the point a hire is made. Posting jobs to external sites is fast with our job distribution tools. Sourcing and screening candidates is easy with a search function that returns candidates in order of relevance. Candidate management and communication is simplified with our intuitive and easy workflow. Real-time reports on source performance enable you to measure your recruitment efforts. We've got it all covered in this single sign-on tool, which integrates with your existing recruiting tools.

In today’s growing HR software market, any candidate management system you choose should add hours back to your day, as well as integrate with other technology partners. Talentstream Recruit strengthens your candidate acquisition strategy and empowers your team members to make the right hires faster.

Features



​​​

Career Site and Sourcing Tools

Our recruitment software converts more job seekers into applicants. We combine a modern career site with resume sourcing and job distribution tools to connect you with candidates on 7,000 job boards and professional networks.

Learn More
​​

One Place, One Search

Bring together past applicants, internal candidates, employee referrals, sourced candidates and talent network members in one recruiting tool that uses our job board search technology to effortlessly find and hire the best candidates faster.
​

Configurable Workflow

Our project managers and engineers will build your platform with simple goals in mind: To make recruitment easy for your team and provide greater visibility for candidates, recruiters, hiring managers and executives. 


​

Automated CRM

Get more out of your applicant tracking system while reducing your overall time to fill and cost per hire. Our candidate recruiting tools use CareerBuilder's proprietary job board technology to re-engage your private talent pool with newly posted jobs.
​​

Data: Planning and Reporting

Elevate your decision-making process with real-time statistics and reports - placing Supply & Demand data  directly within the recruiter’s workflow, tracking success of job boards and social media and much more.     
​

Customer Success Team

Maximize your return on investment with our team-based approach to delivery. Your success team will ensure both a smooth implementation of the recruitment software and provide proactive monitoring to drive user adoption of the tool.
