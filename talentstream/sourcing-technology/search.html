Overview

Simplify your resume search and find candidates faster with CareerBuilder Search. We combine the power of 45 million resumes from the CareerBuilder Resume Database with Recruitment Edge, giving you immediate access to over 150 million candidate profiles with verified contact information.

CareerBuilder’s Recruitment Edge technology aggregates hundreds of millions of public profiles from dozens of online sources — such as online professional networks, social media sites and licensing agencies — then consolidates these profiles into over 150 million unique candidate profiles you can contact via phone or email today.

In the Search Professional Edition, we’ll help you understand how easy or hard it will be to source for a particular position with the seamless integration of Talentstream Supply & Demand data. The information included will provide competitive insights and candidate demographics (including diversity data), as well as suggest other locations or job titles that could be relevant to your resume search.
