Overview

Competition for qualified talent remains at an all-time high, so your organization needs to be strategic when recruiting. To succeed, you need to better understand the changing labor market and workforce analytics, which will shape your organization for years to come.

Emsi Analytics for Talent Acquisition compiles employment and economic data from over 90 data sources into a web-based portal, designed to produce detailed reports in as little as three clicks of a mouse. Emsi Analytics for Talent Acquisition crafts the data into meaningful reports, charts, maps and graphs that tell a consistent story about the labor market (down to the ZIP code level). 

The detail provided in the various data points also delivers demographic information – a crucial component to any diversity initiative. Whether you are looking for candidates with a specific gender, race or ethnicity, Emsi Analytics for Talent Acquisition tell you exactly where to find them.    

Based on historic data and current market trends, this dynamic solution will project labor market growth (or decline) in any given market - identifying areas of opportunity (or improvement) for your business.

Emsi Analytics for Talent Acquisition is part of the Talentstream Technologies suite of solutions that bring together all the tools you need to manage the entire recruitment process.

Features



​

Comprehensive Data Sources

Over 90 federal, state and private data sources updated quarterly and combined for quick analysis.​
​

Workforce Diversity

Demographic information breakdowns by age, gender, race and ethnicity for every occupation in the U.S.
​

Projected Employment

Review 10-year labor market projections and historic economic trends dating back to 2001. ​


​

Job Posting Analytics

Visualize the competitive marketplace, comparing the volume of job postings to the number of monthly hires.  
​

Detailed Geographic Coverage 

Maps and data, available down to the ZIP code level, to track labor supply, demand, and average compensation across the U.S.
