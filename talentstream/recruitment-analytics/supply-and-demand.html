Overview

Talentstream Supply & Demand’s web portal delivers recruitment analytics and insights into both active job seekers and your competitors to help you more effectively recruit in today’s marketplace. The comparisons between these workforce data points demonstrates how easy or hard it will be to attract top talent.

Compensation data within Talentstream Supply & Demand enables you to benchmark the total compensation you are offering against competitor salaries or wages in your local market as well as nationwide.

Use this recruitment analytics tool to develop a targeted recruitment strategy. Gain the insight you need through data sets such as: market comparison, top job posters, active candidates, salary and hourly pay, job posting volume, and job titles used in the marketplace. 

Features



​

Hiring Indicator

Learn how easy or difficult it will be to recruit top talent for your open positions.  
​

Top Job Posters

Identify your top talent competitors and learn what you are up against to recruit qualified candidates.  
​

Compensation Data

Understand how your salary or hourly pay compares to the competition in your market or nationwide.


​

Demographic Data

Benchmark your open job's hiring requirements against candidate qualifications like education, experience and recent job titles.
​

Data Visualization

Display a heat map of your area to narrow your focus on which areas will bring the most candidates, adding efficiency to your sourcing strategy.
​

Staffing-Specific Data

Staffing agencies can use Supply & Demand data to generate sales leads and consult with clients using the latest labor analytics.
