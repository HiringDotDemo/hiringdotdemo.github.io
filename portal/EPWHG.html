All transactions on this site are by paid for at the time of purchase by credit card (Visa, MasterCard or American Express).

Once the purchase is complete, you will be taken to your CareerBuilder account, which is where you can use your products. Jobs can be posted right away and will show up on the site within 24 hours of posting. After purchasing Resume Database access, please allow up to 2 hours for the transaction to be approved by our security team. Any orders submitted outside of normal business hours may not be processed until the following business day. Normal business hours are Monday-Friday 8:30 AM to 8:30 PM Eastern.

CareerBuilder has a variety of application options available. During the posting process, you will indicate whether you want candidates to apply in person, via fax, online (received by you via email), or through your own website or Applicant Tracking System.

You will automatically receive an email containing a PDF of your paid invoice within 2 hours of your purchase. If you do not see the paid invoice in your inbox within 2 hours, please check you junk mail folder. If you are unable to locate the email in your inbox or junk mail folder, please contact our Customer Service Department at 1-800-891-8880.

If you need additional assistance with making a purchase or utilizing our products, you have several resources available to you. First, you can contact your Sales Rep for sales related inquiries or your Client Support Specialist for technical difficulties or training needs. Our toll free Customer Service line - 1-800-891-8880 - is also open from 8:30 AM to 8:30 PM Monday-Friday.
