January 17–23, 2017

Welcome

CareerBuilder invites you to Palm Springs in January for the 2017 CareerBuilder Challenge, one of the most exciting and anticipated Pro-Am golf tournaments of the year. As our guest, you’ll enjoy a rare and coveted opportunity to play alongside six of the best golfers from around the world as they swing for the title. Each day you’ll tee it up with PGA TOUR pros in two-person teams to compete for daily and overall trophies—and top-scoring guests will move on to the final round on Sunday!

In addition to great golf, you’ll enjoy exclusive perks and evening events packed with great food and entertainment. Whether you’re taking part in the fun at our CareerBuilder Challenge Opening Party, Pairings Event and Closing Party or taking advantage of our dedicated Pro-Am concierge, you will experience the finest this beautiful desert resort community has to offer.

Together with our tournament ambassador Phil Mickelson and partners Desert Classic Charities and the PGA TOUR, we’re celebrating the 57th year of this special event and hope you can join us for an unforgettable week.

Matt Ferguson
CEO
